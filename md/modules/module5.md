# Module 5: Who's on First

## Description
A verbal communication puzzle involving a main display and six buttons, all containing words that are easily confused.

## Visual Representation (2D Flat UI)
- **Housing:** A square module panel.
- **Main Display:** A rectangular screen at the top with a word in all-caps (e.g., "READY", "NOTHING", "BLANK").
- **Buttons:** Six rectangular buttons arranged in two columns (Left/Right) of three rows. Each button contains a word.

## Logic & Function
- **Step 1:** Look at the word on the display. The manual says which button position to look at (e.g., "Middle Right").
- **Step 2:** Look at the word on *that* button. The manual provides a list of words for that word.
- **Step 3:** The user must find the first word in that list that also appears on one of the six buttons and press it.
- Incorrect button press results in a strike.

## Clickable Parts
- **Six Word Buttons:** Each of the buttons is clickable.
