MISSION BRIEFING: THE DEFUSER

DO:

  • Communicate everything you see in detail.
  • Rotate the bomb to check for edgework (Serial, Batteries).
  • Keep track of the timer and strikes.

DON'T:

  • NEVER look at the expert's manual.
  • Don't click things randomly; mistakes speed up the clock.
  • Don't panic! Your expert is your only lifeline.

MISSION BRIEFING: THE EXPERT

DO:

  • Navigate the manual quickly using the sidebar.
  • Ask the Defuser for specific details (Serial, Indicators).
  • Give clear, concise instructions.

DON'T:

  • NEVER look at the defuser's screen.
  • Don't forget that the bomb speeds up with each strike.
  • Don't let the Defuser panic!
